Central Dispatch can be connected to BeRocker in just a few steps so your team can start posting loads directly from the CRM.

<Note>
  We value the work Central Dispatch does for the industry and are proud to integrate with their
  platform.
</Note>

## Before You Start

Make sure your Central Dispatch account is on the **Premium** plan.

- The Central Dispatch API requires the **Premium** plan.
- The **Core** plan is not enough for full platform functionality.
- If you are currently on Core, upgrade to Premium before connecting in BeRocker.

## Connect Central Dispatch to BeRocker

<Steps>
  <Step title='Open Integrations in BeRocker'>
    In BeRocker CRM, go to `Settings -> Integrations`.

    In the **Central Dispatch** section, click **Integrate**.

  </Step>
</Steps>

![Central Dispatch Integrate Button in BeRocker](/images/cd/cd_1.png)

<Steps>
  <Step title='Sign In Through Central Dispatch OAuth'>
    After clicking **Integrate**, you will be redirected to the Central Dispatch OAuth login page.

    Enter your Central Dispatch load board credentials and complete sign-in.

  </Step>
</Steps>

![Central Dispatch OAuth Login Page](/images/cd/cd_2.png)

<Steps>
  <Step title='Return to BeRocker and Start Posting'>
    After successful login, Central Dispatch redirects you back to BeRocker automatically.

    Your integration is now complete, and you can start posting to Central Dispatch.

  </Step>
</Steps>
